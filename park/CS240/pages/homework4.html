Ways to attract business to your Garage Sale in Atlanta, GA

  • Ads
  • Entertainment
  • Examples

  1. Advertising

    Advertising is a great way to attract business. Creative advertising is more memorable, longer lasting, works with less media spending

    A Craiglist is a advertisements website with sections devoted to sales, items wanted, services, etc. It's a great spot to advertise your sale. Make sure you plan ahead and detail your inventory in your submission. Facebook sales groups are also a great way to let people know what you have for sale.

    Newpaper ads and signs work well but what happens if your sign falls? What if no one reads the paper this because the world isn't on fire?

  2. Entertainment

    • Spotify, Youtube, etc is easy Entertainment.
    • Make viewing items fun. Adopt an IKEA walk through setup.
    • Make sure everything is setup properly. A TV with a demo of an old console might do just the trick.
    • Be active and entertain your guests. Smaller events allow you to be closer with your customers.

  3. I grew Mike's Garage Sale with these methods.

    At first I used signs and I got some turn out. Signs aren't the easiest to see at times. Better marketing produces better results.

    Give your guests a reason to stay. Hot dogs, cotton candy, and burgers are very small investments but can turn a profit or keep buyers eyeing that one item they want. Try it. Playing some music is cheap as well!
